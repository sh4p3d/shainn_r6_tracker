Here are the working commands!
!r6stats [username] [platform] {ranked}]

You can check your Rainbow Six Siege Statistics such as kills, best mmr and more...

!r6tweet

You can get the latest tweet of the official Rainbow Six Siege Twitter Account.

!ping

You can check the ping of the bot with this command to see if it delays.

!help {command}

You can get help in discord. If you don't write a specific command, it will just list all the commands.
